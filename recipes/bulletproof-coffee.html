Bulletproof Coffee

Description

A delicious alternative to your usual morning brew, bulletproof coffee allows you to skip breakfast by adding butter to your coffee.

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee
  • Unsalted butter
  • Hot water

Steps

  1. Put 1 tsp of instant coffee in blender
  2. Add 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  3. Add 1 cup of boiling water
  4. Blend for 15 seconds
  5. Pour in cup and enjoy
